Who We Are

An Irish company built on trust, since 2002.

The Alliance Credit Company Ltd. has always been an Irish-owned, based and run company. We aim to demonstrate that a home credit service can be a fair and intelligent service by sticking to our principles: simple, friendly, fair and affordable finance.

Over the years, we have built a wealth of knowledge about the credit industry in Ireland and, more importantly, about our customers' expectations. We are built on the concept that the customer is always at the heart of the business.

We encourage our customers to think prudently about their financial situation and train our agents to spend time understanding personal goals — educating them with regards to intelligent borrowing and planning ahead.

New Customers

Cash Loans

As a new customer, you can borrow from €500 to €600 over 36, 43 or 52 weeks at a competitive rate of interest. Loan amounts increase with subsequent borrowing. We offer fixed weekly repayments so you always know exactly what you'll pay.

What You'll Need

Required Documents

To protect both you and us, we require a small number of standard documents before we can process your application. These are required by all regulated lenders in Ireland.

  • Proof of ID — to verify your identity per anti-money laundering regulations
  • Proof of Address (recent) — to verify your current address
  • Proof of Income — within the last 2 months, for affordability assessment
  • Proof of PPSN — to verify you on the Central Credit Register

Responsible Lending

We're Responsible

We are an Irish-owned, based and run High Cost Credit Provider with many satisfied customers. We like to keep the process as simple as possible. We understand that it's not always possible to pay back a loan in large repayments — and that sometimes our customers might need a little longer to repay than originally planned.

We know that the last thing anyone needs when they struggle to repay on time is unexpected additional charges or fees. Our customers like knowing they can speak to someone if they have any questions, queries or concerns. That's why our team is always here for you.

Warning: This is high-cost credit. The Alliance Credit Company Ltd. is a provider of high-cost credit. Consider alternative options before applying, including alternatives from other lenders regulated by the Central Bank of Ireland. Our high cost credit agreements range from an APR of 71.34% with a cost of €30.00 per €100 borrowed, to an APR of 152.67% with a cost of €25.00 per €100 borrowed. If you are struggling to manage your finances, contact MABS at www.mabs.ie for free, confidential advice.
