What We Do

Everything we offer

We keep things simple. One core product, delivered with care and flexibility to suit your needs.

Cash Loans

Borrow from €500 to €600 as a new customer, with amounts increasing with subsequent borrowing. Cash to your door or directly to your bank.

Flexible Repayments

Choose from 36, 43 or 52 week repayment terms. Fixed weekly amounts so you always know exactly what you owe — no surprises, ever.

Personal Agent

Every customer gets a dedicated local agent who visits at your convenience — a real person who knows you by name.

Regulated & Safe

Fully authorised and regulated by the Central Bank of Ireland. Your data and finances are always handled with the highest care.

Fast Decisions

We review every application promptly. Once approved, funds are delivered quickly — in cash or directly to your bank account.

No Hidden Fees

What you see is what you pay. No unexpected charges or penalties. If you need more time to repay, we work with you.
