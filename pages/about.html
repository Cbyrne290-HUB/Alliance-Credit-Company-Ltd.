About Us

Irish-owned, based and run since 2002. Built on the belief that financial services should be simple, fair and human.

Who We Are

A company built on people, not profit.

The Alliance Credit Company Ltd. has always been an Irish-owned, based and run company. We aim to demonstrate that a home credit service can be a fair and intelligent service by sticking to our principles: simple, friendly, fair and affordable finance.

Over the years, we have built a wealth of knowledge about the credit industry in Ireland and, more importantly, about our customers' expectations. We are built on the concept that the customer is always at the heart of the business.

We encourage our customers to think prudently about their financial situation and train our agents to spend time understanding personal goals — educating them with regards to intelligent borrowing and planning ahead.

Our Approach

Lending with a human touch.

Unlike large financial institutions, we don't process applications through automated systems. Every application is reviewed by a real person who takes the time to understand your situation.

Our agents visit customers at home or arrange a time that suits you. We believe this personal approach is what sets us apart — and it's why so many of our customers come back to us again and again.

We are also committed to financial education. Our agents are trained to help customers understand their loans, plan repayments and think carefully about borrowing — so that credit works for you, not against you.

Regulation

Fully Regulated

The Alliance Credit Company Ltd. is authorised and regulated by the Central Bank of Ireland. We operate within all guidelines set out by the Central Bank and adhere to the Consumer Protection Code.

We are a registered High Cost Credit Provider. Loans of €500 or more are registered with the Central Credit Register (CCR).

Warning: This is high-cost credit. Consider alternative options before applying, including alternatives from other lenders regulated by the Central Bank of Ireland. Our high cost credit agreements range from an APR of 71.34% with a cost of €30.00 per €100 borrowed, to an APR of 152.67% with a cost of €25.00 per €100 borrowed. If you are struggling to manage your finances, contact MABS at www.mabs.ie for free, confidential advice.
