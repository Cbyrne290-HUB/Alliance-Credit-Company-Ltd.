Understanding Your Loan

What is APR?

APR stands for Annual Percentage Rate. It represents the total cost of borrowing over a year, including interest and any fees, expressed as a percentage of the loan amount.

Our standard APR is 41.5%. This means on a €500 loan, the cost of credit is clearly shown upfront — there are no additional charges added later.

We are a High Cost Credit Provider as defined by the Central Bank of Ireland. We encourage all customers to consider all available options before applying.
